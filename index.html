FAQ

Answers that help new clients feel comfortable booking.

How often should my dog be groomed?

Most dogs do best every 4 to 8 weeks depending on coat, shedding, and how tidy you like them to stay.

Do you groom puppies?

Yes. Puppy appointments are slower, gentler, and focused on creating a positive first experience.

What if my dog is anxious?

That’s okay. We use a calm approach, take breaks when needed, and prioritize comfort over rushing.

Can I request a specific haircut style?

Absolutely. Inspiration photos are welcome, and we’ll talk through what works best for your dog’s coat and maintenance routine.

How long does a grooming appointment usually take?

Most appointments take about 1.5 to 3 hours depending on your dog’s size, coat, and the service booked.

Do you accept matted dogs?

Yes, but we’ll always talk through the safest and kindest option first. Matted coats may require extra time and start at an additional $35+.

What do I need for my appointment?

Please make sure there is parking available for the truck and mobile trailer, and be ready to complete owner and dog intake details before the visit.

Are there dogs you do not service?

Yes. We do not service aggressive animals, dogs who bite, or dogs with fleas, and we currently only service dogs under 40 lbs.

How do I book an appointment?

You can call, email, or use the booking button on the page. We’ll help you with the intake form, confirm parking access, and find the best time for your dog.
